PARLEVEL STORE

Custom built eCommerce Platform
Description

The Parlevel Store is an eCommerce platform for vending operators and managers to purchase equipment for their vending operation. The Parlevel Store displays products that Parlevel Systems currently offers to their customers that was previously only available by ordering over the phone. With the Parlevel Store, Customers are able to view detailed product information, select the products they need, and then make purchases through our shop interface.

Customers will select the delivery address they would like their products to ship to, and after completing a purchase, will get notified on the estimated time their products will arrive. The Parlevel Store has plans to add many more products for any vending operation, as well as offering some their specialty products such as the Parlevel Micro Market Kiosk.
